# Target Customers

> Who uses PlatsIndex and why.

## Primary Segments

### 1. Homebuyers & Movers

**Profile**: Individuals or families evaluating neighborhoods before purchasing or renting.

**Need**: Objective comparison of areas beyond "gut feeling" and anecdotal advice.

**Use case**: Select two neighborhoods, compare scores across 27 indicators, understand trade-offs (e.g., lower crime but fewer amenities).

**Tier**: Free (explore) → Unlocked (single area purchase) → Subscriber (unlimited access).

### 2. Real Estate Agents & Brokers

**Profile**: Licensed mäklare (Swedish real estate agents) advising clients.

**Need**: Data-backed area reports to complement property listings.

**Use case**: Generate area intelligence for client presentations, identify up-and-coming neighborhoods for listings.

**Tier**: Subscriber (monthly/annual) or Enterprise (brokerage-wide).

### 3. Property Investors

**Profile**: Individual and institutional investors evaluating neighborhood trajectories.

**Need**: Predictive indicators that signal future appreciation or decline.

**Use case**: Screen all 6,160 DeSOs by score trends, filter by urbanity tier, identify undervalued areas with improving fundamentals.

**Tier**: Subscriber → Enterprise.

### 4. Municipal Planners

**Profile**: Kommun-level urban planners and policy makers.

**Need**: Data infrastructure for evidence-based decision-making.

**Use case**: Monitor neighborhood health indicators, compare DeSOs within their kommun, track impact of interventions.

**Tier**: Enterprise with custom weights.

## Enterprise Segment

Enterprise customers can:
- **Customize indicator weights** — Emphasize the factors most relevant to their use case
- **White-label** — Embed PlatsIndex data in their own products
- **API access** — Programmatic access to scores and indicators
- **Tenant isolation** — Custom scoring model separate from the public platform
